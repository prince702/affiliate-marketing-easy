How to make Affiliate Marketing Account

Affiliate Marketing Registration

Join any company you want to affiliate marketing through. Here we will tell you about doing Affiliate Account on Amazon.
Now we know step by step and earn money by creating an account on it.
Step 1. Go To Website
First of all, you should go to https://Affiliate-program.Amazon.In/ website and click on Join For Free option here.

Step 2. Enter Your Details
Here some details will be asked from you, fill it.
• Email or Mobile Phone Number - Enter your email or mobile number here.
• Password - Enter password in it.
• Create Your Amazon Account - After filling your details, click on this option.

Step 3. Fill The Form
Now a form will appear in front of you. Fill all the information asked in it correctly.
• Payee Name - In this you have to fill your name which is in your Bank Account.
• Address Line - In this option you have to write your complete address.
• City - Now write the name of your city.
• State, Province or Region - Write the name of the state here.
• Postal Code - Now fill your Postal Code.
• Phone Number - Fill the phone number and click on Next.

After clicking on Next, you will ask your website. In this, you have to tell the name of your website. If you do not have any kind of website, then you can also write the name of your Facebook page here.

If you do not have a Facebook page, here you can give a link to YouTube channel and if it is not there, then you create a free website and put its link here and click on Next.

After this, a form will come in front of you again. In which you have to give information about your website, but before that you have to fill your Associates Id. After this, you can fill the information of your website.


• How Do You Drive Traffic To Your Website
In this, you have to tell how the visit to your website comes. In this, the option you feel is right - like Blog, Seo, Social Networks or other selections.

• How Do You Utilize Your Websites And Apps To Generate Income?
Here, tell how you were earning before the website.

• How Do You Usually Build Links?
Tell us about how to add a link in the website.

• How Many Total Unique Visitors Do Your Websites And Apps Get Per Month?
Tell how many visitors come to your website every month.

• What Is Your Primary Reason For Joining The Amazon Associates Program?
Now write the reasons here because of which you have joined Amazon Associates Program.

• How Did You Hear About Us?
In this, you have to tell how you got the information of Amazon Affiliate, then you filled it here.

Just now you have to accept the terms and condition by entering captcha and click on Finish to complete this process. After clicking on Finish, your Affiliate Account will be created. Now you are ready to get Amazon Website Products sold.
